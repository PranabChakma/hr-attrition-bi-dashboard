# HR Attrition BI Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project presents an interactive HR Attrition BI dashboard designed to analyze employee turnover and identify key workforce trends. The dashboard helps HR teams understand attrition patterns by age, gender, salary, education, job role, and tenure.

## Tools Used
- Power BI / Looker Studio
- SQL (for data preparation)
- Microsoft Excel / CSV
- GitHub (documentation)

## Key KPIs
- Total Employees: 1.47K
- Total Attrition: 238
- Average Age: 36.92
- Average Salary: 6.50K
- Average Years at Company: 7.01

## Dashboard Analysis
- Attrition by Gender
- Attrition by Age Group
- Attrition by Education Level
- Attrition by Salary Slab
- Attrition by Years at Company
- Attrition by Job Role / Designation
- Employee Satisfaction Impact on Attrition

## Dashboard Preview
![HR Attrition Dashboard](dashboard_images/hr_attrition_overview.png)

## Key Insights
- Highest attrition observed among employees aged 26–35
- Lower salary slabs show significantly higher attrition
- Sales Executive and Laboratory Technician roles have the highest turnover
- Attrition is higher in early years of employment
- Male attrition count is higher than female

## Business Impact
This dashboard enables HR teams to:
- Identify high-risk employee segments
- Improve retention strategies
- Support data-driven workforce planning
